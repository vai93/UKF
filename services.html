OCEAN FREIGHT (IMPORT & EXPORT)

Freight Systems provides a dependable and integrated ocean freight service that puts the customer first. With trained personnel on the ground at ports across the world and partnerships with best-in-class global and regional carriers, we offer a robust and flexible range of global and local Ocean Freight services for both Less-Than-Container Load (LCL) and Full-Container Load (FCL) shipments. ROAD FREIGHT

Road freight is a flexible and efficient overland transport solution suitable for short to medium distances. It offers direct door-to-door delivery, reducing handling time and the risk of cargo damage. With various vehicle options, including trucks, trailers, and specialized transporters, road freight accommodates different cargo types, from perishable goods to heavy machinery. GPS tracking and route optimization ensure timely deliveries while enhancing supply chain efficiency.

The adaptability of road freight makes it a preferred choice for businesses requiring frequent shipments. It supports intermodal logistics, seamlessly integrating with rail, air, and sea transport for comprehensive supply chain solutions. Additionally, cross-border trucking services facilitate smooth international trade by simplifying customs processes and ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements.

RAIL FREIGHT

Rail freight is a cost-effective and environmentally friendly transport solution for long-distance shipments. It is ideal for moving bulk commodities, including industrial goods, construction materials, and agricultural products. Rail transport significantly reduces fuel consumption and carbon emissions compared to road and air transport, making it a sustainable logistics option. With fixed schedules and dedicated rail networks, rail freight ensures reliable and secure transportation.

A key advantage of rail freight is its ability to handle large volumes efficiently, reducing congestion on roadways and minimizing delays. It also integrates well with multimodal transport solutions, connecting seaports, road networks, and distribution centers for seamless cargo movement. Businesses seeking a balance between cost efficiency and environmental responsibility often rely on rail freight as a strategic logistics solution.

PROJECT CARGO

Project cargo involves specialized handling and logistics solutions for oversized, heavy, or complex shipments. Industries such as oil and gas, construction, energy, and manufacturing frequently require project cargo services for transporting large equipment and industrial machinery. This service includes route planning, securing necessary permits, and using heavy-lift equipment to ensure safe transport.

Each project cargo shipment is tailored to specific requirements, involving expert coordination across various transport modes. Factors such as weight distribution, load security, and compliance with international regulations are meticulously planned to minimize risks. With end-to-end management, project cargo services provide seamless execution for complex logistics projects, ensuring timely and safe delivery.

AIR CHARTER

Air charter services provide customized air transport solutions for urgent, high-value, or specialized shipments. Unlike scheduled air freight services, air charter allows businesses to book dedicated aircraft, ensuring immediate departure and direct delivery to the destination. This service is ideal for time-critical cargo, emergency relief supplies, and high-priority shipments requiring exclusive handling.

With flexibility in aircraft selection, air charter accommodates diverse cargo needs, from perishable goods to industrial equipment. The ability to bypass congested airports and optimize flight routes makes air charter a reliable option for businesses seeking fast and secure transportation solutions. Personalized logistics planning ensures smooth operations, minimizing delays and maximizing efficiency.

EXHIBITION CARGO

Exhibition cargo services specialize in the transportation and handling of trade show and exhibition materials. These shipments often require precise timing and careful handling to ensure displays, promotional materials, and equipment arrive intact and on schedule. Dedicated logistics teams manage packaging, customs clearance, and on-site delivery to event venues worldwide.

With experience in international exhibitions, this service provides seamless logistics coordination from the point of origin to the exhibition center. Secure handling, specialized transportation, and temporary warehousing solutions ensure exhibitors can focus on their presentations without worrying about logistics challenges. Post-event return shipping services further simplify the process for businesses participating in multiple exhibitions.

CUSTOMS CLEARANCE

Customs clearance services facilitate smooth import and export operations by handling regulatory compliance, documentation, and duty payments. Trained customs brokers ensure shipments meet all legal requirements, preventing delays and penalties. By managing paperwork, tariff classifications, and customs duties, businesses can navigate international trade seamlessly.

An efficient customs clearance process reduces transit time and enhances supply chain reliability. With expertise in handling various types of cargo, including restricted and regulated goods, this service ensures hassle-free cross-border trade. Automated systems and electronic filing further expedite customs processing, making international shipping more efficient.

CROSS BORDER ROAD SERVICES

Cross-border road freight services provide seamless transportation for shipments moving across international borders. This service ensures compliance with customs regulations, trade agreements, and security protocols, enabling efficient cross-country trade. Dedicated trucking solutions allow for the transport of diverse cargo, including perishable goods, industrial products, and retail merchandise.

By leveraging well-established logistics networks and customs clearance expertise, cross-border road services minimize transit delays. GPS tracking, real-time monitoring, and integrated logistics planning ensure smooth and secure transportation. Businesses benefit from reduced costs and improved efficiency when shipping goods across multiple countries.

INCOTERMS

Incoterms (International Commercial Terms) define responsibilities between buyers and sellers in global trade transactions. Expertise in managing shipments under different Incoterms, such as EXW (Ex Works), DDP (Delivered Duty Paid), DDU (Delivered Duty Unpaid), FOB (Free on Board), and C&F (Cost and Freight), ensures compliance and clarity in shipping agreements.

Proper understanding and implementation of Incoterms help businesses mitigate risks, control costs, and ensure smooth logistics operations. By clearly defining the transfer of liability, shipping responsibilities, and payment terms, Incoterms facilitate transparent and efficient international trade transactions.

MULTIMODAL LOGISTICS

Multimodal logistics services integrate multiple transport modes—road, sea, air, and rail—to optimize supply chain efficiency. This approach enables businesses to leverage the advantages of each transport method, reducing costs and transit times while ensuring seamless cargo movement.

By offering a single point of coordination, multimodal logistics simplifies complex shipping requirements. Advanced tracking systems, synchronized schedules, and optimized routes enhance cargo visibility and reliability. Businesses benefit from greater flexibility and efficiency in global trade operations.

COURIER & CARGO

Courier and cargo services provide fast and reliable transportation solutions for domestic and international shipments. These services cater to a wide range of needs, from express document delivery to bulk cargo transportation. With options such as same-day, next-day, and economy shipping, businesses can choose solutions that match their urgency and budget.

Advanced tracking, secure packaging, and efficient customs handling ensure safe and timely delivery. Whether for e-commerce, healthcare, or corporate shipments, courier and cargo services offer convenience, flexibility, and reliability for businesses and individuals alike

WAREHOUSING & DISTRIBUTION

Warehousing and distribution services offer secure storage solutions and streamlined supply chain management for businesses. Modern warehouses are equipped with advanced inventory tracking systems, climate control, and high-security measures to protect goods. These facilities support various storage needs, including short-term and long-term inventory management, cross-docking, and fulfillment services.

Efficient distribution networks ensure timely order processing, packaging, and last-mile delivery. Companies benefit from optimized logistics operations that reduce costs, improve inventory turnover, and enhance customer satisfaction. By integrating warehousing with transport solutions, businesses can maintain a seamless supply chain and respond effectively to market demands.
